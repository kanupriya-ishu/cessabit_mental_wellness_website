Cessabit

We provide help related to mental health problems for free of cost. You can discuss your problem through our "Discuss" section and if you want to feel better on your own, use our "Self-Help" section to explore different articles, meditation and quizes. We hope you feel better after exploring our website!

Our Story

Everyone in this world faces mental health issues at some point of their life and they need help to overcome these problems. It is seen that individuals refuse to speak with friends or family about mental wellbeing. Moreover, professional help is very expensive and everyone cannot afford it. Many individuals will never be able to solve their mental health challenges if this issue is not resolved.

To overcome this problem, we are building a website where anyone can communicate about their mental health issues through a form and we will get back to them with a solution. For self-help, one can browse through our resources which includes quiz, articles and meditation videos.

What mental health needs is more sunlight, more candor, and more unashamed conversation.
